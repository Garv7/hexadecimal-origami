Camel

1.Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
2.Camels can reach 7 feet in height (at the hump) and weigh up to 1500 pounds.
3.Camels have quite small ears but good hearing and the ears have hairs inside to help keep the dust out

Chameleon

1.There are around 160 species of chameleon.
2.Chameleons are not deaf but they do not actually have ear openings.
3.Species of chameleon can be as small as 15 mm (0.59 in) or as large as 69 cm (27 in).
